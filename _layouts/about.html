The Basics

The DC SCORES Cup is the largest annual charity soccer tournament in the Washington, DC, area and one of DC SCORES' large fundraisers. The World Cup-style, round robin co-ed tournament, created in 2003, pits local law firms and corporations against each other in a fun, competitive environment.

The one-day tournament is held each June at the beautiful Maryland SoccerPlex in Gaithersburg, MD, and has grown each year â€” to a record 40 teams in 2017. Almost 90 percent of all monies raised directly benefits the DC SCORES program for 2,500 low-income youth.

The DC SCORES Cup has been highlighted as an effective team-builder in Capital Community News, Legal Bisnow and Washington Business Journal.
