The Basics

The DC SCORES Cup is the largest annual charity soccer tournament in the Washington, DC, area and one of DC SCORES' largest fundraisers. The World Cup-style, co-ed tournament, created in 2003, pits local law firms and corporations against each other in a fun, competitive environment.

The one-day tournament is held each June at the beautiful Maryland SoccerPlex in Gaithersburg, MD, and has grown each year â€” to a record 46 teams in 2018. Money raised directly benefits the DC SCORES program for 3,000 low-income youth.

The DC SCORES Cup has been highlighted as an effective team-builder in Capital Community News, Legal Bisnow and Washington Business Journal.
