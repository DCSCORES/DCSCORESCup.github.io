Soccer for good

The DC SCORES Cup is the largest annual charity soccer tournament in the region. This year, 42 teams and over 600 participants took the field for bragging rights and to support a great cause. Re-live the 2019 DC SCORES Cup with our recap and photo album.

About DC SCORES

DC SCORES creates neighborhood teams that give kids in need the confidence and skills to succeed on the playing field, in the classroom, and in life.
